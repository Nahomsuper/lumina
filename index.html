Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know about our products

Lumina fixtures are crafted from hand-spun brass, frosted crystal diffusers, and premium-grade metals with a brushed or aged finish. Each component is selected for its light-transmission properties and durability, ensuring warm, directional illumination that lasts for years.
PureSip juices are cold-pressed and flash-pasteurized at low temperatures to preserve maximum nutrient density while ensuring safety. No heat-degraded flavours, no additives, no preservatives. They retain the natural enzymes and vitamins found in fresh organic fruit.
Ethiopian top-grain leather is prized for its tight fibre structure, natural grain texture, and exceptional durability. Our Artisan Leather products use eco-safe vegetable-tanning processes that avoid heavy metals, resulting in a rich patina that develops character over years of use.
Yes, all Earth & Oven sauces, preserves, and gourmet grains are certified gluten-free. Our kitchens maintain strict segregation protocols to prevent cross-contamination. Every batch is tested and labelled with full nutritional and allergen information.
Absolutely. Elara Hair products are formulated with gentle Ethiopian botanicals suitable for straight, wavy, curly, and coily textures. Our range includes sulphate-free shampoos, cold-pressed oils, and deep-conditioning masks that work beautifully on natural, relaxed, and colour-treated hair.
Yes, we ship worldwide. Orders are dispatched from Addis Ababa within 2–3 business days. International delivery takes 7–14 business days depending on the destination. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout, and duties or taxes may apply based on your country's regulations.
We offer a 14-day return policy for unused and unopened products. For Lumina fixtures and Artisan Leather goods, you have 7 days after delivery to notify us of any defects or damage. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days after we receive the returned item.
Within Addis Ababa, delivery takes 1–2 business days. For regional cities and towns, expect 3–5 business days via our partnered courier services. All orders above 2,000 ETB qualify for free domestic shipping.
Ethical sourcing is at our core. We work directly with small-scale Ethiopian farmers, artisans, and cooperatives, paying fair wages and supporting local communities. All raw materials — from shea butter to organic fruit — are traceable and sustainably harvested.
Yes, we welcome wholesale and bulk orders. Volume discounts are available for PureSip juices, Earth & Oven gourmet foods, and Elara Hair products. Please reach out through our contact form with your estimated quantity, and our team will send a custom quotation within 48 hours.
Every Lumina lighting fixture is backed by a 2-year manufacturer's warranty covering material defects and workmanship. The warranty excludes bulbs, normal wear, and damage from improper installation. Keep your purchase receipt for any warranty claims.
Yes. PureSip uses 100% recyclable PET bottles and eco-friendly packaging. We encourage customers to rinse and recycle after use. We also offer a bottle-return programme in Addis Ababa — return 12 empty bottles and receive a discount on your next order.
Clean with a soft, dry cloth and condition every 3–4 months with a natural leather balm. Avoid prolonged exposure to direct sunlight or moisture. Store in the included dust bag when not in use. Proper care ensures your leather piece develops a beautiful, unique patina over time.
No artificial preservatives, colours, or flavours are ever used. Our products rely on traditional preservation methods — natural acidity, vacuum sealing, and small-batch production. Once opened, refrigerate and consume within 7–10 days for the best taste and quality.
Yes! PureSip and Earth & Oven both offer flexible subscription plans — weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. Subscribers enjoy 10–15% off each order, free delivery, and the ability to pause or cancel anytime. Manage your subscription easily through your account dashboard.
